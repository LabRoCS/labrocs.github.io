About the group

Our main research subject is Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, encompassing topics such as Attention, Computer Vision and Reinforcement Learning applied to control. Our work heavily uses recent developments in Deep Learning.

Our group is diverse, composed of people with different backgrounds (applied maths, control/electrical/computer engineering, computer science etc), and of different levels (from undergrads to PhD students).
