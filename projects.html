Marta

Marta is a humanoid robot with 25 degrees of freedom and $110 cm$ of height. It has an Intel NUC Mini-PC for processing and two $14.8 V$ - $4400 mAh$ batteries. For sensors, there is a Logitech C920 HD camera and one Inertial Measurement Unit UM7-LT with nine-axis. The actuators are Dynamixel MX-64T and AX-12.

It presents some important characteristics that differ from most of other robots such a small contact area in the foot, extra degrees of freedom in the foot, and an actuated spherical joint in its waist, which has special importance for this project.
